# StellarApp Employee Guide

StellarApp is a platform for **employees** who want to:

- find real jobs in Spain and Europe  
- build a transparent professional reputation  
- collect reviews and recognitions they can reuse in the future  

Official website:  
https://www.stellar-app.net

Spanish main landing:  
https://www.stellar-app.net/es

---

## What can employees do in StellarApp?

- Create a **professional profile** (experience, sectors, skills).  
- Get **verified reviews** from final customers and employers.  
- Apply to **job offers** published inside the platform.  
- Participate in a **shared room** with employers and recognised employees.  
- Stand out as **employee of the month** or **employee of the year**.
